Course
Building Blocks of Music
Length 4 weeks
Dates Fall 2021
Cost $250
Designed for the music lover who is eager to enhance their listening experience by developing a vocabulary which will enable them to understand and describe what they are hearing.
The course will consist of highly engaging videos as well as live lectures.

In this level you will learn about the Elements of Music:

Pitch scales and intervals

Rhythm meter and tempo

Harmony major and minor

Timbre sound qualities of various instruments and voice types

Form common song structures
